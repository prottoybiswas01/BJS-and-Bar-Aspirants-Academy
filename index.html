Student Portal Workflow Sheet Blueprint
Built for judiciary, bar, and legal drafting students

A cleaner student portal for your coaching center, with YouTube classes and Google Sheets control.

This version is structured so one student can access two, three, or more courses at the same time. You decide the assigned courses in a Google Sheet, and the portal automatically shows only the relevant classes, notices, and schedule.
